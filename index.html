Testimonials

Dr, Andrew Johnson

Transitioning from academia to working as a cell assay scientist in biotech has been a game-changer for me. Unlike the slow pace and limited resources in academia, biotech offers a dynamic environment where my work directly accelerates drug discovery and development. I love the clear sense of impact and the collaboration with cross-functional teams, where my research is not just theoretical but contributes to real-world therapies. The faster pace, cutting-edge technology, and seeing tangible outcomes make this career far more fulfilling than academia ever was.

Dr, Zara Galici

Moving from academia to UX design has been an amazing journey. I love applying my research skills to create user-centered solutions, and seeing how my work improves people's experiences is incredibly fulfilling. I've found a perfect blend of creativity and problem-solving that keeps me inspired every day!

Dr, Ron McDonald

Transitioning from academia to working as an application scientist has been such a positive change for me. In this role, I get to work hands-on with cutting-edge technology, helping people solve real-world problems every day. Unlike the slow, isolated pace of academia, I love the variety and collaboration, where I can see the immediate impact of my work. It's been so much more rewarding to apply my skills in a way that feels dynamic and connected to real progress.
